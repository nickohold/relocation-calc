What we collect

Relocation Architect runs entirely in your browser. The salary, rent, and pension figures you type are not transmitted to any server, stored in any database, or shared with any third party. They live only in your tab's memory and disappear when you close it.

We use Vercel Analytics and Vercel Speed Insights to measure aggregate, anonymous traffic and performance. These services collect:

  • Page views and route changes
  • Approximate location (country / region — derived from IP, not stored)
  • Browser, OS, and device type
  • Page load and rendering performance metrics

Vercel Analytics is cookieless and does not track individual users across sessions or sites. See Vercel's analytics privacy policy for details.

What we do not do

  • No accounts, logins, or user profiles
  • No advertising or third-party trackers
  • No selling, sharing, or licensing of data
  • No persistent cookies set by this site
